## Radiance look and feel - toned down skins

### Business

Available in the `BusinessSkin`. In addition, the `RadianceBusinessLookAndFeel` can be used with one of the following options:

* `RadianceLafCortex.GlobalScope.setSkin(new BusinessSkin())`
* `RadianceLafCortex.GlobalScope.setSkin("org.pushingpixels.laf.api.skin.RadianceBusinessSkin")`
* `-Dswing.defaultlaf=org.pushingpixels.laf.api.skin.RadianceBusinessLookAndFeel`
* `UIManager.setLookAndFeel(new RadianceBusinessLookAndFeel())`
* `UIManager.setLookAndFeel("org.pushingpixels.laf.api.skin.RadianceBusinessLookAndFeel");`
